# Lightmist Coffee 微嵐咖啡

A small, family-run coffee project based in Taipei, focused on curating clean, modern light-roast coffees from outstanding independent roasters around the world.

We source small-batch lots — sealed and imported in limited quantities — from roasters whose interpretation and precision bring out more nuance than what's typically available locally. Every drop ships with the water recipe we used to dial it in.

---

**What we care about:**

- **Clarity over intensity.** Ultra-light roasts where origin character speaks clearly.
- **Water as a variable.** Brew water has a bigger impact than most gear. We publish our recipes.
- **Transparency.** Sourcing details, process notes, resting guidance — all shared openly.
- **Community.** We run the Taipei Coffee Group on Discord — a space for people who enjoy discovering something special together.
